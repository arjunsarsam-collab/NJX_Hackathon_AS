      Import an email from Gmail

      In Gmail: open the email, select the three-dot menu next to Reply, then choose Download message.

      Upload the downloaded .eml file below. It is analyzed in your browser and is not uploaded or stored.

      Why can’t I simply forward it to this website?

      A website cannot receive forwarded email by itself. Direct forwarding requires a dedicated mail server and inbound email address. This version gives the same analysis while keeping the email on your device.

      How SafeCircle works

      SafeCircle checks for common scam patterns such as urgency, threats, unusual payment requests, secrecy, suspicious links, impersonation, and requests for passwords or verification codes.

      The checker runs locally in your browser. Screenshot text extraction uses Tesseract.js in your browser. No message history is uploaded or stored by this app.

      Because scammers constantly change tactics, a low-risk result does not prove that a message is safe.
